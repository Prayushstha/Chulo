About KhajaTime

Serving Authentic Nepali Flavors Since 2018

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Bhaktapur in 2018, KhajaTime began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of Nepali cuisine to food lovers across the valley. What started as a small family-run eatery has grown into one of Bhaktapur's most beloved dining destinations.

Our name "KhajaTime" reflects our philosophy that every meal should be a celebration. We combine traditional Newari cooking techniques with modern culinary innovation to create dishes that honor our heritage while delighting contemporary palates.

Today, we proudly serve over 500 customers daily, offering both dine-in experiences and convenient online ordering for those who want to enjoy our food from the comfort of their homes.
